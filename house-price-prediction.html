House Price Prediction

Impact of the Project

This machine learning project aims to predict house prices accurately by analyzing various features of residential properties. Such a tool can have significant real-world applications, including:Â 

  • Assisting real estate buyers or investors in mortgage approvals and property valuations.
  • Supporting financial institutions in assessing the value of properties.
  • Guiding urban planners and policymakers in understanding key factors influencing housing markets.
  • Helping buyers and sellers make informed decisions by providing fair price estimates.

Key Insights

The model identified the most influential factors in determining house prices, such as:

  1. Overall quality of materials and finishes.
  2. Living area square footage.
  3. Number of fireplaces and full bathrooms.
  4. Garage size and exterior material quality.

Leveraging advanced techniques like log transformation and feature selection, the project improved model accuracy while offering actionable insights. This demonstrates how machine learning transforms data into actionable insights, benefiting the real world property industry.
